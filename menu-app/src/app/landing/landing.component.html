1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

TabVerse uses cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

These are required for the platform to function properly. They include:

  • Authentication tokens (JWT) to keep you logged in
  • Session identifiers for maintaining your active session
  • Tenant identification for multi-restaurant data isolation
  • Theme preferences (light/dark mode)

Functional Cookies

These enhance your experience by remembering your preferences:

  • Language and display preferences
  • Cart items for customer ordering sessions
  • Last visited pages and navigation state

Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics tools to understand how users interact with the platform. This helps us improve features and user experience. No personally identifiable information is shared with third-party analytics services.
